Pizza recipe

Description

This recipe is a quick way to win everyone over. It takes approximately 10 minutes to prepare and is very easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Various toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Prepare the pizza dough by mixing flour, yeast, water, olive oil, and salt.
  3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to desired thickness.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Add mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from oven, let cool slightly, slice, and serve.
