hotdog

Description

This hotdog recipe is quick and perfect for casual meals. It takes about 15 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Hotdog buns
  • Hotdog sausages
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Relish
  • Onions
  • Cheese slices (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the sausages by boiling, grilling, or pan-frying.
  2. Warm the buns in the oven or toaster.
  3. Place the hotdog sausage inside the bun.
  4. Add mustard, ketchup, relish, and onions as toppings.
  5. Add cheese slices if desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy.
