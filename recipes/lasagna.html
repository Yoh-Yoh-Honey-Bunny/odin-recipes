lasagna

Description

This lasagne recipe is hearty and perfect for family dinners. It takes about 1 hour to prepare and bake.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Cook lasagne noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a pan, sauté garlic and onion in olive oil, add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce and herbs to the beef mixture and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for 40-45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
